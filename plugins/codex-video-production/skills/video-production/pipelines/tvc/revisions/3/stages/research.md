# TVC — Research

## Stage goal

Produce sourced product, category, audience and competitor research that directly supports defensible TVC concepts.

## Required inputs

Read the confirmed `brief.md` and relevant entries in `assets/index.json`. Use real research tools and current sources; distinguish client claims, published facts and Host inference.

## Domain instructions

Research the product and promise, target audience tensions, category codes, major competitors, platform context, creative opportunities, compliance risks and likely differentiation. Prefer primary or authoritative sources. Every material finding must cite a source ID and explain its creative implication.

## Output contract

Write `research.md` with clear sections for product facts, client claims, inferences, audience, category, competitors, opportunities, risks and creative implications. Write `research-sources.json` as an array of `{id,title,url,accessedAt,evidenceClass}` records. Do not paste long source text.

## Review and completion

Commit both files and complete after source IDs resolve and the research is sufficient to create meaningfully different proposals. No routine user Review is required.

## Boundaries

Do not write proposals, scripts, Units, storyboards or Provider prompts, and do not generate media.
